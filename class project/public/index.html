Home Page: New York Restaurant Inspection

Inspiration:

We thought it'd be interesting to look at the data for restaurant inspections in New York City and try to find what type of restaurant (pizza, cafe, etc) has the best/worst health inspections, or what parts of NYC have the healthiest/least healthy restaurants. If possible and time permitting, we'd like to incorporate Yelp reviews and see how customer experiences compare to health inspections.
Our data source: https://data.cityofnewyork.us/Health/DOHMH-New-York-City-Restaurant-Inspection-Results/43nn-pn8j
